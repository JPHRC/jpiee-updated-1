    • Transport

      At Jaiprakash Institute of Education and Erudition, institute understand that easy and reliable transportation is essential to ensure that students can focus on their education and practical training without the hassle of commuting. That's why institute offer comprehensive transportation services to meet your needs.

      Hostel to College: Provide dedicated and secure transportation services for students residing in our college hostels. Well-maintained buses and vans run on established routes to pick up and drop off students, ensuring a safe and comfortable journey to and from the college.

      College to Hospital: As a student, you'll spend a significant amount of your time at hospitals and healthcare facilities for hands-on training and clinical experience. Institute offer transportation to and from these sites, making it easy for you to attend your clinical rotations and gain practical exposure in real healthcare settings.

      Educational Tours and Hospital Visits: Throughout your academic journey, you may have opportunities for educational tours, hospital visits, and field trips as part of your curriculum. Institute arrange transportation for such occasions to ensure that you have easy access to these valuable learning experiences.

      Our transportation services are known for their reliability, safety, and punctuality. We have a team of experienced and licensed drivers who prioritize your safety and comfort during your journey.

    • HEALTH BENEFITS TO STUDENTS

      At Jaiprakash institute of Education and Erudition, Institute dedicated to not only providing a top-notch education but also ensuring the overall well-being of our students. We believe that a healthy mind and body are essential for academic success and personal growth. That's why we offer a range of health benefits to support our students as well as their NOKs throughout their academic journey.

      Medical Dispensary facility

      At Jaiprakash institute of Education and Erudition, your health and well-being are of ulmost importance to us. We are dedicated to providing a safe and supportive environment for all our students. To ensure your health needs, we offer comprehensive first aid and dispensary facilities on campus.Our well-equipped first aid stations are strategically located throughout the campus to provide immediate assistance in case of minor injuries, illnesses, or emergencies. Trained staff is available to administer first aid and offer guidance on appropriate medical care.


      Annual Health check-up

      The annual health check is designed to ensure that you are physically and mentally prepared for the rigors of education. It's a proactive step to identify and address any potential health concerns early, allowing us to provide you with the necessary support and guidance. The admission health check-up provided to you free of charge, which includes medical history review, physical examination, essential medical tests, health consultation, etc.

      Hospital discount policy at JPHRC

      Jaiprakash institute of Education and Erudition, have established a comprehensive Hospital Discount Policy that extends to both our students and their Next of Kin (NOK) at Jaiprakash hospital and research centre. This policy reflects our commitment to making quality healthcare more accessible and affordable for our college community.
